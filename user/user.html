Explore Cuisines

Discover a wide variety of cuisines from different cultures! From Asia to Europe, CookITHow helps you master it all.

Jollof Rice / West Africa

A vibrant, spiced rice dish cooked in a flavorful tomato-based sauce, often served with chicken or fish.

Tagine / Morocco

A slow-cooked, aromatic stew featuring meats, vegetables, and spices, traditionally prepared and served in an earthenware pot.

Bobotie South Africa

A savory baked dish with spiced minced meat topped with an egg-based custard, offering a delightful blend of sweet and savory flavors.

Injera with Doro Wat / Ethiopia

Injera is a spongy, sourdough flatbread used to scoop up Doro Wat, a rich and spicy chicken stew simmered with berbere spice and clarified butter.

Suya / Nigeria

Skewered and grilled meat, often beef or chicken, heavily spiced with a dry peanut-based rub, delivering a smoky and nutty flavor.

Chili con Carne

A hearty stew made with chili peppers, meat (usually beef), tomatoes, and often beans, offering a warm and spicy flavor.

Asado / Argentina

AA traditional South American barbecue, featuring various cuts of meat (especially beef) slow-cooked over an open flame or embers, renowned for its smoky flavor.

Ramen / Japan

A Japanese noodle soup dish, with a rich broth, wheat noodles, and various toppings such as sliced pork, nori, and green onions.

Lumpia / Philippines

Filipino spring rolls, commonly filled with ground meat and vegetables, then deep-fried until crispy, served with dipping sauce.

Fish and Chips / United Kingdom:

A popular takeaway dish of battered and deep-fried fish (usually cod or haddock) served with thick-cut fried potatoes.

Paella Spain

A vibrant Spanish rice dish originally from Valencia, typically made with rice, saffron, vegetables, and seafood or meat.

Biryani / India

A flavorful mixed rice dish made with spices, rice, and meat or vegetables, known for its aromatic layers and rich taste.
